# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 20, 2025**

ppippi-dev Blog ("this site") values the privacy of its visitors and complies with applicable privacy laws. This Privacy Policy describes the information this site collects and how it is used.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Automatically Collected Information

This site uses Google Analytics to automatically collect the following information:

- Visitor IP addresses (anonymized)
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referrer information
- Approximate geographic location

### 1.2 Cookies

This site uses cookies for the following purposes:

- **Google Analytics**: To analyze website traffic and understand user behavior
- **Google AdSense** (planned for future use): To provide personalized advertisements

Users can block or delete cookies through their browser settings. However, blocking cookies may limit some functionality.

## 2. Purpose of Information Use

The collected information is used only for the following purposes:

- Improving website performance and user experience
- Statistical analysis for content quality improvement
- Analyzing website traffic and usage patterns
- Ad serving (when Google AdSense is implemented in the future)

## 3. Third-Party Services

### 3.1 Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to analyze website usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about users' website usage. This information is anonymized and cannot identify individuals.

- Google Analytics Privacy Policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Opt out of Google Analytics: [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

### 3.2 Google AdSense (Planned for Future Use)

This site may display advertisements through Google AdSense in the future. Google AdSense uses cookies to provide ads tailored to users' interests.

- Google AdSense Privacy Policy: [https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

### 3.3 GitHub Pages

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub's privacy policy can be found at:

- GitHub Privacy Statement: [https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement)

## 4. Information Storage and Protection

This site securely stores collected information and does not sell or arbitrarily share it with third parties. Information collected by Google Analytics and Google AdSense is processed according to their respective privacy policies.

## 5. User Rights

Users have the following rights:

- Right to refuse cookie usage
- Right to block Google Analytics tracking
- Right to opt out of personalized advertising

## 6. Children's Privacy

This site does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 14 years of age.

## 7. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be modified in accordance with changes in relevant laws and policies. If there are changes, they will be announced through this page.
